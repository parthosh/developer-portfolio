Teachers' self-reported assessment practices and the influence of teaching experience, grade level, and subject area on those practices were explored in this study. Questionnaires were administered to 893 teachers in 34 schools. Teachers reported using performance and observation-based assessment methods most frequently and had the most confidence in the validity of these assessment techniques.
